All codes are 8 digits.

For the spare parts, they always start with 3 (1st digit).

Unlike raw materials, the main category is always fixed: 50 قطع غيار الماكينات
(it is not taken from digits 2–3 of the code).

The next 2 digits after that block (4th, 5th) represent the subcategory, and the last 3 digits represent the item itself.

For example, these are spare parts from subcategory 01 عدة بانش:

30101001
30101002
30101003

These are spare parts from subcategory 02 دايات بانش:

30102001
30102002
30102003

These are spare parts from subcategory 04 قطع كهربائية وهيدروليكية وحساسات:

30104001
30104002
30104003
